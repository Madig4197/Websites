1.1.9 Project: Build a Snowman!

In Build a Snowman users will be prompted with a variety of questions that will aid them in designing their own Snowman.

1.2.5 Project: Reindeer Run!

In Reindeer Run users will have to cross over barriers with moving objects to get to the other side. If they are hit by one of the moving objects they will lose a life. If you survive to the end of the game you win.

1.3.1 Scratch Project

In Sandwich Shop players will act as employees and will fulfill the orders of the customer by clicking on the different ingredients to make the customer’s sandwich. If players are successful in making the Sandwich then they’ll receive coins!
